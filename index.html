TOP 1

Tanjung Gelam Beach

Arriving at this beach, you can be sure that you will not regret it. The location is filled with amazing beauty. The stretch of white sand that covers the coast is still very clean, adding to the beauty of this beach. Of course, this variety of beauty will make you feel more at home there. Whether morning, afternoon or evening, the Karimunjawa tourist destination Ujung Gelam Beach is still beautiful. Of course it gets more beautiful when evening comes.

TOP 2

Annora Beach

A beach destination that displays a very beautiful beach view. You can see the blue water of the Karimunjawa sea from the height of Anora Hill, like the Kelingking beach in Lombok. Anora Beach is quite far from the city center of Karimunjawa. If you visit it, it will take 4 days and 3 nights while in Karimunjawa. This means you have to have extra time to explore Anora Beach.
